Warehouse Manifesto

The idea is to grow the brand as a vinyl label in the coming years. I also want to host my personal favourite DJs/mixers to contribute to a podcast that represents the underground sound of techno and electro that I have loved for over 20 years. Soundcloud has provided me with a platform to showcase my mixes through my own sets and a multitude of podcasts, meeting some great people in the process. It is good to give back as well, so this is my chance. Some great mixes coming out over the next few months so stay connected. Warehouse Manifesto advocates tolerance, acceptance and kindness to all.
